Welcome to Rufflets

Not a chain. A family home you can stay in.

Rufflets was built in 1924 as a private house by the architect Donald Mills, and became one of Scotland's first country house hotels. The Russell family have looked after it since 1952, and they still do, in person, today.

Nature, shared. Hospitality from the heart, in intimate spaces made for being together.

Our Story

Three generations. One front door.

Built in 1924 by the architect Donald Mills as a private home, Rufflets became one of Scotland's first country house hotels. The Russell family took it on in 1952, and have run it personally ever since, bringing people together for over seventy years.
